About Me

My favorite comforts include naps on rainy days, tea-flavored ice cream, and proving why s'mores are terrible in reality. After recently graduating from UC Berkeley, I'm embracing the unknown and seeking opportunities in the creative field. As a business major, I learned how perception is influenced by the way language is employed. And as a designer, I am exposed to a world of visual story-telling that doesn't rely on words. I am passionate about work that operates in the overlap between both worlds. Feel free to contact me here.

Experience & Projects

Take a look at my linkedin, resume & portfolio (and its case studies).

Caliber Magazine

Caliber Magazine is a student-run magazine that covers a variety of topics relevant to the Berkeley community. I served as Head of Design in 2018 and 2019, where I helped oversee the execution of some really neat issues.

The Daily Californian

The Daily Californian is the city of Berkeley's paper of record, with an average of 3.7 million website views per month. I joined in 2017 as a layout designer and eventually became the Creative Director, where I managed all of the paper's print and online visual content. I also wrote a column on some of my experiences there.

Misc Projects

I like to stay busy in my free time. This has led to various leisure projects like a presentation on why Sebastian from Disney's The Little Mermaid cannot possibly be a crab, or this very website.
